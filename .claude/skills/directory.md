## Privacy & Surveillance Awareness

*Source: [GetFlockOut Linktree](https://linktr.ee/GetFlockOut) — "What is Flock? What can I do?"*

### What Are Flock Cameras?

Flock Safety cameras are automated license plate readers (ALPRs/LPRs) deployed across neighborhoods, often without community consent. They track vehicle movements and feed data to law enforcement.

- **Flock's Creepy Surveillance System** — [YouTube explainer](https://youtu.be/0Mc3MTpkZEQ)
- **How Unsecure & Widespread is Flock?** — [YouTube](https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=Pp9MwZkHiMQ)
- **Flock Camera Leak: Netflix for Stalkers** — [YouTube](https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=vU1-uiUlHTo)
- **404 Media Flock Coverage** — [404media.co/tag/flock](https://www.404media.co/tag/flock)
